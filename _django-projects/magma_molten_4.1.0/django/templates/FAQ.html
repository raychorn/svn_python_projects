MOLTEN FAQ
The purpose of this FAQ is to provide user information about Magma's MOLTEN site
How to connect to MOLTEN site, What are browser requirements?

To connect to MOLTEN site, point your web browser to:

http://molten.magma-da.com

Your browser needs to support cookies and allow JavaScript and Java.

If your browser doesn't allow JavaScript and Java you will not be able to post comments and attach files to cases.
How do I login to MOLTEN? What Username / Password should I use?

Generally, your email address is your Username, Password is generated for you during registration process.

If you don't have a MOLTEN account, you should request one by registering:
go to MOLTEN home page, select "sign Up" (https://molten.magma-da.com/contact/signup)
Once we receive your registration request we will process it in next 24 hours.
You will receive an email with your account information.

If you forget your password, you can always retrieve it by clicking on "Forgot Your Password?"
Password will be sent to your email address.
Where do I report problems with MOLTEN site?

Most of the MOLTEN errors are self-detected!

If such exception occurs, you will see on the web page minimal information about the error and action that will take place. MOLTEN will also generate and display on the web page a unique Support ID #.

You can Support ID # reference in the future correspondence with Magma support team in regards to the error that occurred. When error occurs, you don't need to do anything except log the Support ID for your reference.

MOLTEN support team will receive the email with error details and they will start working on a resolution ASAP. Once problem is solved you will receive an email from MOLTEN support team..

You can also report problems with the site by sending email to molten-support@magma-da.com
Can I open a new case or add comments to existing case using only email?
Yes, you can create new MOLTEN case using only email. Also, you can add comments to existing MOLTEN case using only email. Here is how:

a. To open new case or to add comment to existing case, send email to sfmolten at magma-da.com

b. The subject of the email will become the subject on the case.

c. (Optional) By specifying the version number, product, component, and operating system of the product, the email to case functionality has the ability to automatically populate those respective fields in the system, thus utilizing the case assignment rules.

To use this feature enter the field name followed by a colon and then enter the value you want for this field. Each field should be on it's own line.

d. An acknowledgment will be sent back once the email is converted to a case.

e. The email subject will contain a reference tag at the end of the subject line.

Example:
RE: Thank you for filing your Case [ ref:00D32QQ.500332evu:ref ]

f. You can then reply to this email. Any replies to this email will be added as a case comment to the case that this corresponds to.
