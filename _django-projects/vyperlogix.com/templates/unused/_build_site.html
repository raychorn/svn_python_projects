Time to craft the content for your site.

You will notice an icon that looks like , click on it to edit the content in the area where this icon has been placed.

There is one icon for the header and another for footer of your site. You may replace the content for your header and footer by clicking on the icon.

The remaining pages of content will appear within the center area of the site between the header and footer.

There are two kinds of Pages you may add to your site. One kind of Page is tied to the Menu Bar, the other is not.

When you add a Page that is tied to the Menu Bar a button will be added to your Menu Bar. There will always be 3 buttons on your Menu Bar by default; the Home button, the Login button and Register button.

The other Page type allows you to added Content Pages your visitors will access by clicking on a link that leads to one of these pages.

When you see an icon that looks like you may click it to Add a page to your site.

When you see an icon that looks like you may click it to Change the current page from being a Page that is tied to the Menu Bar to one that is not or vice-versa. You may click the icon to remove a Menu Bar Button from the Menu Bar and vice-versa.

When you click the "Edit Site" button you will be shown a pop-up window with the aforementioned icons that allow you to edit your site's content.

When you click the "View Site" button you will be shown your site just as those who visit your site will be shown.
